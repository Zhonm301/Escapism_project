Exit
After the recall of memories,
Alice was no longer awake. She had forgotton
her identity, her emotions, her memories,
her life.
In the snow, she layed.
A warmth covered her during the
night and snow.
As time passed,
I felt a numbness in my brain,
as it prevented me from remembering-
slowly even losing my train of thoughts.
Alice is no longer part of the meaning of existance.
There is nothing left in the
the world for her to do.
The only thing left
is the wind.
And snow,
hitting her skin as she lay in the same spot.
... This is the escape of Alice.
